# Key Insights from Customer Churn Analysis

## 1️⃣ High Churn in Germany
Germany shows the highest churn rate compared to France and Spain.

Possible reasons:
- Customer dissatisfaction
- Competitive banking services

---

## 2️⃣ Product Usage Impacts Retention
Customers using only one product have significantly higher churn rates.

Customers with two products show the strongest retention.

Business recommendation:
Encourage cross-selling of banking products.

---

## 3️⃣ Inactive Customers Are High Risk
Inactive customers show a much higher likelihood of churn.

Recommendation:
Implement engagement campaigns for inactive users.

---

## 4️⃣ Middle-Aged Customers Dominate Customer Base
Most customers belong to the middle-age segment.

However, churn occurs across all age groups.

---

## 5️⃣ Significant Financial Risk
Churned customers hold approximately **185M in balance**.

This indicates a major potential revenue loss.

Recommendation:
Focus retention strategies on high-value customers.
